# Portugal Wedding

Wedding trip to Zambujeira do Mar, Portugal — May 11, 2026. Friend's wedding. Evaluating family (2 adults + Ruben 4yo + Cyril 1yo) vs solo trip options.

## Key Facts
- Wedding: May 11 (ceremony), May 12 (chill day)
- Location: Zambujeira do Mar, Alentejo coast, SW Portugal
- Best airport: Lisbon (LIS) — 2h40m drive
- Return: May 12 evening or May 13 morning

## Domain Learnings
- Fly to Lisbon, not Faro (no useful direct PRG-FAO flights)
- easyJet PRG-LIS is cheapest (~150 EUR/adult RT), TAP is more comfortable (~220 EUR)
- Family budget ~1,310 EUR / 32,750 CZK; Solo budget ~377 EUR / 9,425 CZK
- Zambujeira is tiny — book accommodation ASAP
- Bring own car seat for Cyril (free check-in on easyJet/Ryanair)
