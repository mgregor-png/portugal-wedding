## Geography & Logistics Overview

Zambujeira do Mar is a small coastal village in SW Alentejo, Portugal. It's remote — no nearby commercial airport.

| Airport | Distance to Zambujeira | Drive time | Notes |
|---------|----------------------|------------|-------|
| **Faro (FAO)** | ~147 km | ~1h 40m | Closer, but fewer direct flights from Prague |
| **Lisbon (LIS)** | ~220 km | ~2h 40m | More flights, better connections, direct options |
| Beja | ~100 km | ~1h 15m | No commercial flights |

**Recommendation: Fly to Lisbon.** More direct flight options from Prague (TAP, easyJet daily), better pricing, and only ~1 hour more driving than Faro. Faro has only 1 weekly Eurowings direct flight (Saturdays), which doesn't align well with the schedule.

## 2. GROUND TRANSPORT (Airport -> Zambujeira do Mar)

### Option A: Rental Car (RECOMMENDED for family)

#### From Lisbon Airport

| Category | Price/day | 3-4 days total | Notes |
|----------|----------|---------------|-------|
| Economy (Fiat 500, Clio) | 20-35 EUR | 60-140 EUR | Too small for family with kids |
| Compact (Golf, Focus) | 30-50 EUR | 90-200 EUR | OK for solo/couple |
| Intermediate/SUV | 40-65 EUR | 120-260 EUR | Good for family with car seats |
| Minivan | 50-80 EUR | 150-320 EUR | Most comfortable for family |

**Additional costs:**
- Child car seat rental: 5-10 EUR/day (or ~30 EUR flat from independent rental like Little Rascals)
- Booster seat (Ruben): 5-8 EUR/day
- Fuel: ~30-40 EUR round-trip (Lisbon-Zambujeira ~440 km total)
- Tolls: ~15-20 EUR (A2 motorway has tolls; alternative via N roads is toll-free but slower)
- Insurance excess reduction: 10-15 EUR/day (recommended)

**Tip:** Book via DiscoverCars or RentalCars for best aggregated prices. Avoid airport surcharge by taking a shuttle to city center rental — saves ~15-30 EUR.

#### From Faro Airport (if using Eurowings Saturday flight)
- Similar pricing but ~1h shorter drive
- Fuel: ~20-25 EUR round-trip
- Fewer tolls

### Option B: Bus (good for solo)

**Rede Expressos / FlixBus — Lisbon Sete Rios -> Zambujeira do Mar**
- Duration: 2h 55m - 3h 15m
- Price: 17.50 EUR one-way / 31.60 EUR return
- Frequency: 1-4 departures/day (May is shoulder season, expect 2-3/day)
- Departs from Sete Rios bus station (metro accessible from airport)

**Solo verdict:** Bus is viable and cheap. For family with 2 small kids + luggage — not practical.

### Option C: Private Transfer
- Lisbon airport to Zambujeira: ~200-250 EUR one-way
- Only makes sense if splitting with other wedding guests

## 6. KEY LOGISTICS CHALLENGES & TIPS

### Family-specific challenges
1. **Car seats:** Bring your own car seat for Cyril (1yo) — rental ones are hit-or-miss quality. Ryanair/easyJet allow free check-in of car seat + stroller per child. Rent a booster for Ruben from the car rental company.
2. **Stroller:** Free to check on all airlines. Can use until aircraft steps.
3. **Timing with kids:** The 2h40m drive from Lisbon can be tough with small kids. Plan for a stop. Consider arriving May 9 (Fri) to have a buffer day.
4. **Accommodation:** Book NOW. Zambujeira is tiny and wedding guests will snap up inventory.
5. **Nap schedule:** If wedding is afternoon/evening, plan for kids' nap logistics.

### General tips
1. **Flight booking:** easyJet opens bookings ~6 months ahead. May 2026 should be bookable now. Compare on Google Flights or Skyscanner.
2. **Car rental:** Book via aggregator (DiscoverCars, RentalCars). Full-to-full fuel policy. Get excess insurance separately (iCarhireinsurance.com is ~30 EUR total vs 10-15 EUR/day from rental company).
3. **Driving:** Portugal drives on the right. A2 motorway to Alentejo, then local roads. Last stretch is winding coastal roads — beautiful but slow.
4. **Tolls:** A2/A6 have electronic tolls. Rental car should have Via Verde device — confirm at pickup.
5. **Bus backup:** If solo and flights arrive late, Rede Expressos last bus is typically early evening. Check schedule.
6. **Weather:** May in Alentejo = 20-25C, sunny, occasional rain. Perfect.
7. **Phone/data:** EU roaming from Czech SIM works fine.

### Savings opportunities
- **Fly midweek** if possible (Tue/Wed departures are cheapest)
- **Self-catering** for breakfasts/lunches saves significant money with family
- **Share transport** with other wedding guests if any are coming from Prague
- **Off-airport car rental** in Lisbon can save 15-30 EUR on airport surcharge
