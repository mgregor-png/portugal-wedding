place About Zambujeira do Mar

Tiny fishing village on the dramatic Alentejo coast. Part of the Rota Vicentina hiking trail network. Famous for the Sudoeste Festival (not in May). Beautiful wild beaches, cliff walks, fresh seafood. May weather: 20–25°C, sunny.
